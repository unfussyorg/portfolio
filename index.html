OpenSpace
Mobile / Tablet
ID
360° reality capture with mapped metadata
Abstract
OpenSpace worksites accumulate piles of geo-located content: field notes, change requests, messaging. All of it inaccessible while doing a capture walk.

I designed a capture experience that surfaces this content at the right place and time.

A path trace keeps track of what's been covered in a capture session. We can alert to a photo request as the user approaches the right zone. Unresolved field notes can be updated along the way, instead of requiring a separate walk.

ID
CV-guided reality capture
Abstract
As Hover expanded from pros to homeowners, new users frequently bounced during their first capture. ARCapture creates a visual feedback loop between the user's camera & our CV backend, ensuring beginner's luck for everyone.

We took advantage of LIDAR & on-device CV reconstruction to guide homeowners towards a good capture with no blind spots.
