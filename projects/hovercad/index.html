HoverCAD is human-first, AI assisted 3D modeling. Starting with a blank sheet, we slashed Hover's unit cost by 75% in the first two years.

Core features include interactive photogrammetry for solving camera networks, CV surface texturing & ML-powered feature recognition.

We started with a core suite of parametric tools, taking advantage of deep knowledge of our user's process in other modeling apps.

We used property data, local code, & CV to predict gutter locations & paths while a user draws. Automation only makes suggestions when they're likely to be helpful.
